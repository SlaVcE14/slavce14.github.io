Privacy Policy for JSP Now

Last updated: April 25, 2026

JSP Now is committed to protecting your privacy. This policy explains how our application handles information.

Information Collection and Use

JSP Now does not automatically collect, store, or share any personal identity data. The app is designed to provide public transport information with minimal data footprint.

Location Data

Our app requests access to your device's location to provide the "My Location" feature on the map and to show nearby bus stops. This location data is processed locally on your device and via Google Play Services. We do not store or track your location history on our servers.

Device Information

The app may access basic device information (such as screen size and model) only for display optimization. This information remains on your device.

Third-Party Services & Libraries

JSP Now utilizes several industry-standard services to provide its functionality:

  • Google Maps SDK: Used to display the transit map. Google may collect anonymous usage data as part of their service.
  • OkHttp & Retrofit: Used to fetch real-time bus locations and schedules from transit APIs.
  • Room Database: Used to store your "Favorite" stops and lines locally on your device. This data never leaves your phone.

Data Sharing

The app does not share user data with any third parties, except for the necessary communication with transit APIs to fetch the data you request (e.g., bus arrivals).

Children’s Privacy

JSP Now is intended for a general audience and does not knowingly collect information from children under the age of 13.

Third-Party Disclaimer

JSP Now is an independent, third-party application. It is not an official app of JSP Skopje. We are not responsible for the accuracy of the data provided by the transit authorities.

Changes to This Policy

We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page.
